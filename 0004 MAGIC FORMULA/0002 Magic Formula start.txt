Financial and Utility companies are excluded
MktCapM is >= US$ 300 million (basis year 2000) adjusted yearly
Rank all stocks according to latest filing Return on Capital (ROC)
Rank all stocks according to latest filing Earnings Yield (EY)
Take the 200 stocks with the smallest sum [rank (ROC) + rank (EY)]

	Return on capital = EBIT / NOA.

PREÇO JUSTO: Earnings yield = EBIT / Enterprise Value.
	Return on capital = EBIT / EV.
